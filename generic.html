About Me

Roots

Born and raised in the Bay Area the west coast will always be home and a significant part of forming my perspective on the world. My family is originally from the island of Grenada and in my experiences of visiting more family it's broadened my view and appreciation for multiple aspects of life.

University/School

I did my undergraduate schooling at the University of California - Berkeley earning a B.A degree in Data Science. I was enrolled for 4 years from 2016 to 2020 and participated in various groups, organizations, research, and fellowships such as BESSA, BGESS, Fung Fellowship, and more. I continued my education at Berkeley completeing my graduate degree from 2021 to 2023 studying Data Science & Information Technology. The projects on the landing page are part of the body of work I completed.
